Mabie Industries

Verifier-backed AI systems for expert workflows.

Mabie Industries is the business-facing surface for my applied AI systems work: local and open-source model deployment, systematic evals, agent harnesses, post-training loops, structured artifact AI, and cognitive-security review.

The thesis is simple: borrow model capacity, own the problem shape, and compile discoveries back into tests, validators, datasets, interfaces, and workflow constraints.
